Looking forward to the Expo Coming Up!!

My first post! The countdown begins! I can't wait for you guys to look at all my recently painted paintings.

Extract Written by GalwayNow Magazine "Catherine has been painting from a very young age, holding her first exhibition at the mere age of 11 along with aunt Rena Fleming in aid of the Burren Chernobyl Project. Since then she has had several exhibitions around Galway. The theme behind the majority of Catherine’s pieces is anxiety and portray the often overwhelming experience of panic through the bold colours and patterns surrounding the figures in her paintings. Her work demonstrates visually what so many experience and struggle with throughout their daily lives whilst suffering from anxiety."

For more information, see Catherine Fleming Artist on Facebook or Instagram-@catherineflemingart
