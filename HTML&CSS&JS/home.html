About Me

From a very young age I began to develope an interest in painting, holding my first exhibition at the mere age of 11 along with my aunt Rena Fleming in aid of the Burren Chernobyl Project. Since then I have had several exhibitions around Galway. The theme behind the majority of my pieces is anxiety and portray the often overwhelming experience of panic through the bold colours and patterns surrounding the figures in my paintings. I hope that my work demonstrates visually what so many experience and struggle with throughout their daily lives whilst suffering from anxiety."
